Raleigh Daniels Jr

Raleigh Daniels Jr (also known as MegaRdaniels) is an Atlanta native, Supernatural Novelist and Comic Book Writer. A lifelong, animated storyteller, Raleigh enjoys crafting stories with absolute, gruesome, pulp-fiction esque graphic violence and thrilling adventures inspired by the old tales of Penny Dreadful and The League of Extraordinary Gentlemen. When he is not writing, he likes to enjoy heavy metal bands such as Disturbed, punk rock, such as Green Day, and—well various forms of soundtracks from Two Steps from Hell to Immediate Music.
